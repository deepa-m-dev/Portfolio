About Me

"I don't just write code — I build systems that think, adapt, and solve."

I'm Deepa M, a Software Engineer and BCA student at Vels University (CGPA: 9.3/10), passionate about building AI-powered applications that solve real-world problems with precision and purpose.

My journey started in a Commerce classroom — where logic, numbers, and analytical thinking shaped how I see the world (95.8% overall, with a centum in Accounts & Commerce). That foundation followed me into software engineering, where I now combine full-stack development and machine learning to build systems that are not just functional — but intelligent.

I work across the full stack — from training ML models with Python, Scikit-learn, and XGBoost, to building backends with Flask and Spring Boot, to crafting clean frontends with React and JavaScript. My projects include HireAI (an AI recruitment intelligence system), UrbanFlow AI (a traffic prediction platform), and a Smart Observability Platform — each built end-to-end and portfolio-ready.

Beyond engineering, I'm also a poet and visual artist. Creativity isn't separate from how I code — it shapes how I design, communicate, and think through problems.

I'm currently open to SWE and ML Engineer opportunities where I can build, learn, and grow alongside driven teams. Let's build something impactful. 🚀
